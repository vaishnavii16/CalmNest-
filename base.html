About Us

CalmNest is a mental health solution that aims to provide users with easy access to resources related to mental wellbeing.

  • Here users can play brainstorming games and fun quizzes which will help them learn in a fun way. Yoga and Exercises are very important to reduce stress levels, which is also available on our website.

Blogs

Therese Bouchard is a mental health advocate and writer, and has been named a top 10 influencer in the mental health community. Her blog covers mental health and holistic health, with topics that include depression, generalized anxiety disorder, and nutrition.

Therese Bouchard

Talkspace is one of the top mental health blogs around. The service aims to make therapy accessible and affordable, and shares useful and practical advice and tips on how to manage mental health through their blog.

Bisma Anwar

Chipur is a blog dedicated to helping people with anxiety, depression, and bipolar through learning. The blog was founded by Bill White, a mental health professional, and features hundreds of articles on mood disorders and the science behind them.

Bill White

Purple Persuasion is a mental health blog by Charlotte Walker, a service user with bipolar disorder. Charlotte created the blog to connect with others in similar situations, and it documents her journey and thoughts on living with bipolar.

Purple Persuasion

The Blurt Foundation is a social enterprise that’s dedicated to helping people with depression. Over on the blog, the Blurt team shares insights into living with depression and anxiety, as well as practical tips like meal planning when you have low energy.

Blurt Team
